The perfect carbonara recipe

Carbona, an italian classic. You can't go wrong with this one!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 100g of parmesan, shredded
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 100g of bacon
  • some salt for the extra taste
  • 400g dried spaghetti or linguine
  • 1dl of boiled water

How to make the perfect carbonara

  • Mix eggs and parmesan in a boil
  • Boil the pasta in slightly salty water. Save 1 dl of the boiled water.
  • Fry the bacon until crisp in a dry pan. Add oilve oil and the boiled pasta.
  • Add the mixture of eggs and parmesan. Add some of the boiled water.
  • Add some salt and taste.
  • Add some pepper before serving.
